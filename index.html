All-in-one

More often than not one's road to maximal technological
literacy, at least computer-optimization-wise is a multi-year
process including countless different sources from Youtube,
the internet, you name it! It's such a hassle!

At OPTMZ we know this. Being technologically literate isn't
just slight interest in the components of a PC when you sit
in front of it. It's an instant rush to optimize any system
you sit in front of. The passion of making sure everything,
temperatures, thermal conductivity, airflow, power efficiency,
OS-level performance is optimized properly.

Not nearly enough people possess this strong of a passion
to making sure the very things that'll run our society
for centuries are properly optimized both for longevity
and perfomance.

We aim for a better future. Join us! OPTMZ!
